# Food Trends Understanding Customer Preferences in FB
## Project Overview
This project presents a comprehensive analysis of nutrition, dietary behavior, and lifestyle patterns through a set of interactive Power BI dashboards. It explores how daily food choices, meal distribution, physical activity, and health conditions collectively shape nutritional intake and overall well-being. The dashboards are built to deliver clear, data-driven insights through dynamic visualizations and filters, allowing users to interact with the data, identify trends, and better understand the relationship between lifestyle decisions and health outcomes.
## Objectives
- Analyze dietary patterns across different meal types
- Evaluate nutritional intake (calories, protein, fat, carbohydrates, sugar)
- Understand the impact of exercise levels and lifestyle habits
- Study relationships between diet and health conditions (BMI, diseases)
- Compare diet types (Vegetarian, Vegan, Non-Vegetarian)
- Provide data-driven insights for healthier lifestyle decisions
## Dashboard Overview
### 1. Dietary Preferences & Averages
Focus: Understanding eating habits, meal distribution, and nutrient intake
## Key Insights:
- Lunch and dinner have the highest food and calorie intake.
- Nutrient intake is fairly balanced across meals.
- Water intake peaks at dinner.
- Calories and protein show a positive correlation.
- Sugar values are mostly low but show occasional spikes across cholesterol levels.

### 2. Nutrients and Diseases
Focus: Relationship between diet, health conditions, and lifestyle
## Key Insights:
- Average BMI indicates a tendency toward the overweight range.
- Calorie intake remains relatively consistent across exercise levels.
- Vegetarian diets contribute the highest overall BMI share, followed by non-vegetarian.
- Health scores are higher for moderate and low sugar intake groups.
- Exercise level has a noticeable impact on BMI distribution.
- Strong connection between diet type, sugar intake, and health outcomes.

### 2. Averages by Breakfast Suggestions
Focus: Impact of breakfast choices on calorie intake and lifestyle
## Key Insights:
- Breakfast options show significant variation in calories and protein.
- Higher activity levels are associated with higher calorie intake.
- Calorie intake generally increases with weight.
- Most individuals follow omnivore and vegan dietary preferences.
- Breakfast choices contribute noticeably to overall daily calorie intake.

### 4. Lifestyle and Nutrition Impact
Focus: Combined effect of lifestyle, nutrition, and diseases
## Key Insights:
-Average calorie intake aligns closely with daily calorie targets.
- Protein intake varies significantly with activity level.
- Extremely active individuals consume higher protein and sugar levels.
- Weight is positively associated with calorie and nutrient intake.
- Gender-based differences in calorie consumption are minimal but noticeable.
- Multiple health conditions are linked with dietary imbalance and lifestyle factors.
